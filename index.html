Our Story

Family. Faith. Music.

Kimberly Kofoed is the mom — and now grandma — of a big, beautifully messy family, and a songwriter who believes music is one of the most powerful ways we connect with each other and with God. Kofoed Family Music grew out of that belief: that a song can lighten a burden, open a heart, and remind you that you are never, ever alone.

Every track is written from the inside of real life — the hard moments and the joyful ones — and sung by a family that knows what it means to hold each other up. We make music so you feel seen. You are loved.
