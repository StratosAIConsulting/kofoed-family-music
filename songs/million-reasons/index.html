Million Reasons

Kofoed Family Music · Single

Stream or Download

Spotify Apple Music
“Heavenly Parents? Eternal Families? A purpose for being here? Yes!! There are truly a ‘MILLION REASONS’ why I believe.”

Last fall I was in New York City about to cross a busy street, when 2 beautiful women came up to my friend and me and asked, “Do you know that we have a Heavenly Mother and scriptures witness of it?”. I answered “Yes!” I wanted to share more, and I could tell they did too, but we had to catch up with our group.

I have 7 daughters and 10 granddaughters, and I want them to know that they are created in the image of their Heavenly Parents, that they are divine, and they are loved! I believe, particularly as women, we need to acknowledge that more and look for all the things that witness of this truth.

In the process of writing this song, I was filled with a great sense of awe about our Heavenly Parents: who they are, who we are, and the incredible purpose of why we are here. I felt a glimpse of our Eternal Family, with a compassionate Heavenly Father AND Mother and their children… each of us here!

This plan is so AMAZING… so ASTONISHING… and there are truly a “MILLION REASONS” why I believe.

“There’s a Father pleading we’ll know He’s near, there’s a Heavenly Mother reaching out her hand… to feel their love and understand.”
